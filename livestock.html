ABOUT PIG

Choosing a pig is not just a matter of taste. We want you to be happy with your purchase.
We offer the best pork, Our pigs live in spacious pens and eat lush, organic feed. Our pigs have a diet rich in vegetables, which provide high nutrition. The feed they eat is fresh, organic and free of chemicals, they don't get any antibiotics or growth hormones. We have an extensive selection of purebred pigs available, including Hampshire , WERTHAM and alpacas. All our pigs live in a free range environment with plenty of sunlight and fresh air.


Moreover; If you're looking for a pig who gets along well with others, is social and playful, but doesn't need much time outside the pen, look no further than our friendly and fun pigs. Their calm temperament makes them great for those who prefer not to have a lot of excitable hogs running around their yards. We offer home delivery on most of our pigs.
